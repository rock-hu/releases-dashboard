## changes
10.3.8 - May 2026

* (PW-3202) Fix: removed spurious <code>Error propagating pending prefix mapping</code> warnings when parsing regular ISO 20022 Documents wrapped in an SNL-like envelope
* (PW-3185) <code>MxSwiftMessage.toJson()</code> now uses 1-based months (January=1) for Calendar fields and emits a <code>schemaVersion</code> marker. <code>fromJson()</code> reads both new and legacy (0-based) payloads transparently.
* Updated gson from 2.13.2 to 2.14.0
